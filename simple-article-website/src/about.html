Who We Are

Cybersecurity Awareness for Seniors is dedicated to helping senior citizens stay safe online. Our mission is to provide simple, practical, and up-to-date information about cybersecurity threats and how to avoid them.

Our Goals

  • Make cybersecurity easy to understand for everyone
  • Share tips to prevent scams, fraud, and online threats
  • Empower seniors to use technology with confidence
